Website Planning Document

Impact Digital Academy

Site Name

Impact Digital Academy

This name was chosen because it clearly and professionally identifies the business. "Impact" communicates the goal of creating meaningful change in students' careers, "Digital" specifies the industry, and "Academy" positions it as an educational institution. It's memorable and instills confidence.

Optional Domain Availability: impactdigitalacademy.com

Site Purpose

The website for Impact Digital Academy will serve as the primary digital hub for the business. Its purpose is to:

  • Clearly articulate the academy's mission, values, and unique teaching philosophy.
  • Showcase the available courses with detailed descriptions, prerequisites, and outcomes.
  • Generate leads by providing an easy way for potential students to request information or enroll.
  • Build trust and credibility through student testimonials and a blog with industry insights.

Scenarios

These are examples of questions a potential student (our target audience) might have when visiting the site:

  • "I'm a marketing manager looking to upskill. What specific skills will I learn in the 'Digital Marketing Mastery' course, and how will it help me get a promotion?"
  • "I'm a career changer with no tech background. Do you offer any beginner-friendly courses, and what kind of support do you provide for job placement after graduation?"

Color Schema

The following color palette will be used throughout the site:

  • Primary Color: Prussian Blue (#003366) - This deep, trustworthy blue will be used for the header, footer, primary buttons, and main headings.
  • Secondary Color: Bright Cerulean (#007bff) - This vibrant blue will be used for accents, hover effects on links and buttons, and secondary headings.
  • Text Color: Jet (#333333) - This dark gray will be used for the primary body text to ensure high readability.
  • Background Color: Cultured (#f8f9fa) - This off-white will be used for the page background to reduce eye strain.

Typography

The following fonts will be used on the website:

  • Heading Font: Poppins - This modern, geometric sans-serif font will be used for all headings (H1, H2, H3, etc.). It conveys a clean and professional feel.
  • Body Font: Open Sans - This highly readable, neutral sans-serif font will be used for all paragraphs, lists, and other body text. It pairs perfectly with Poppins.
